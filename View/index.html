So, What is ?

Just Imagine a platform where you can say whatever you want as anonymous, just be yourself at this time. We have the rights to express our feelings without worried about others judgement.

uSay!t provides the free spaces for all of you to say your words. With only a few clicks and the words will be sent to various social media.

Keep it Safe with uS !

We expected that users comply with the terms of service policy on each social media platform that we provide. To ensure safety on uSay!t, we filter some words that are harsh, offensive, harmful, bullying, and others. The system stores users personal data in case of a violation. Posts will also be deleted automatically by the system or manually by the admin if the message contains things that are not supposed to.

Contact us regarding safety on octatech.id@gmail.com
